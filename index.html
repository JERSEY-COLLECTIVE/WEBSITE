Info

Debut invasion party in an unsuspecting basement where the acts and audience "broke into" a home to celebrate the new year. A live feed security camera recorded and was played back to the audience, forcing the crowd to look back at themselves. A series of posters with varrying themes and suggestions were created for the event.
